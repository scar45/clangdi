• These are some demos available for download. All were played by ClanGDI members.

  • To play a demo, extract it to your /demos directory in what Mod directory the demo is in. For example, a Rocket Arena 2 demo would go in the Quake2/arena/demos directory. While in the console, type "map db.dm2" or whatever demo you want to view.
